And the spirit became flesh
Religion has a way of reaching into all corners of the human condition: how did the spirit become flesh, become human? The problem of becoming human is one we all face first thing every morning; for my part I find that a nice strong cup of tea usually does the trick. Works for me. Why is it supposed to be such a mystery?
