Positively the Last Word On Wittgenstein

Awful fellow: never stopped talking!

Paul Dirac

This reached me thru' the Cambridge oral tradition (i think it was Jeremy Butterfield who told me).
Dirac of course was famously taciturn. I never met him, tho' my mother knew his wife.
