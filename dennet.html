``The title of Hubel and Wiesel's article should not have been
``What the frog's eye tells the frog's brain'';
—it should have been
``What the frog's eye tells the frog''.''

Dan Dennett
Brainstorms p 13.

It's a very good remark, and it would make a wonderful undergraduate essay question.
