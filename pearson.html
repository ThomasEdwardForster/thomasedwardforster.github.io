Going to heavan

``When I die I want my ashes scattered on Lake Taupo. That way I know I'll go to heavan eventually''.

Supri Soengkono

Soengkono is a geophysicist, so he is well aware (as you, Dear Reader, might not be) that Taupo is the caldera of a supervolcano. And one day it will go off again.
